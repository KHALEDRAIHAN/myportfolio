About Me

KMR
Who I Am

Khaled Md Raihan

I'm a professional photographer and cinematographer based in Bangladesh, with over 8 years of experience capturing the world through a cinematic lens. My work spans concert stages, corporate boardrooms, and film sets — always with the same singular goal: finding the frame that tells the truth.

What started as a late-night obsession with a borrowed camera has grown into a full-time career covering Bangladesh's biggest music acts, leading brands, and independent film productions. I've had the privilege of working with artists like Artcell, Tahsan, and Rahat Fateh Ali Khan, alongside major corporations across various industries.

Every assignment is approached with meticulous preparation and an intuitive eye for authentic moments — the kind of images that don't just document, they resonate.

Philosophy

My Approach

🎯

Intention Before Execution

Every shot begins with a question: what story does this moment need to tell? I spend as much time thinking as I do shooting.

💡

Light Is the Language

Whether natural or artificial, available or crafted — understanding light is the fundamental skill that separates good photography from great photography.

⚡

Speed Meets Precision

In live environments, you get one chance. I've trained myself to anticipate moments, not react to them — so the decisive frame is never missed.

🎨

Consistent Visual Identity

Every brand, artist, and client gets a look that is authentically theirs — developed through collaboration, refined through post-production.
